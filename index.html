...where being evil is not debated!

Behold! Some of the most evil inators!

Ugly-inator

Are you tired of not being handsome? Even if your doctor says it's genetic, don't blame your parents. Blame everyone else for being better-looking that you! The Ugly-inator harnesses the horned frog's unpleasant appearance to render it's target ugly!

Copy-and-Paste-inator

Do you hate waiting in line? All the time wasted in stores, and amusement parks, where you could be spreading more evil! The Copy-and-Paste-inator allows you to make copies of yourself, and they will do the waiting for you.

Voice-inator

Do you have a high, squeaky voice? Not anymore. The Voice-inator bio-mechanically transforms normal air into Doofelium, which will make everyone else's voice higher, making your voice deeper by comparison.

Slave-inator

Don't want to celebrate your birthday alone? The crowd will do whatever the Slave-inator tells them to do! It even has a Cleanup party button, because the worst part of a party is the cleanup, isn't it?

Evil doesn't always have to be on a big scale. You can spread evil in the little things you do everyday.
